About LNCT

The LNCT Group stands as a prominent and diverse education conglomerate in Central India, known for its excellence in academia and its contribution across multiple domains. It encompasses esteemed institutions such as Lakshmi Narain College of Technology (LNCT), LNCT University, Jai Narain College of Technology (JNCT) and JNCT Professional University, and LNCT Vidhyapeeth University, among others, offering courses in engineering, medicine, pharmacy, nursing, management, and more.

Additionally, the group includes specialized colleges like LNCT Excellence, LNCT College of Pharmacy, LNCT College of Nursing, Rishiraj College of Dental Sciences and Research Centre in Bhopal, Indore LN Medical College, and LN Ayurved College, which further expand its footprint in professional education. Extending beyond higher education, LNCT World School provides a nurturing environment for school-level learning.
